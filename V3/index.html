About Krypto

  Krypto started out as BeffsBrowser, back when I was in my early years of Highschool, when I got my first download I was so excited of what I had just done. And here we are some time later with a complete remake. Krypto is designed to be open, and increase productivity with the simple things first. And most of all, Krypto is open source and built using open source.
